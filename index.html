Spaghetti Carbonara

Preparation Time: 10 minutes

Cooking Time: 15 minutes

Total Time: 25 minutes

Servings: 2

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, fry the pancetta until crisp.
  3. Whisk the eggs and Parmesan together in a mixing bowl.
  4. Drain the pasta, reserving some cooking water.
  5. Toss the pasta with the pancetta, then remove from heat.
  6. Quickly mix in the egg mixture, adding reserved pasta water if needed for creaminess.
  7. Season with salt and pepper. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Nutrient Amount
